Our Journey

A timeline of innovation and growth

2010

Company Founded

TechNova Solutions was established with a vision to revolutionize the technology industry. Started with a small team of 5 passionate engineers in a garage.

2013

First Major Product Launch

Launched our flagship IoT platform, serving over 100 enterprise clients. Expanded team to 20 members and opened our first office.

2016

International Expansion

Opened offices in Europe and Asia. Reached 500+ clients globally and launched our AI/ML division with cutting-edge research capabilities.

Launched our green technology initiative, committed to carbon-neutral operations. Introduced eco-friendly product line and renewable energy solutions.
